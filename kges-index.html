KGES' Personal Naratives

What do the youth truly know about living?

Frankly, absolutely nothing.

The majority of us fumble through life, often unable to tell right from wrong. Over and over again, we stagger in school, we falter in love, we break in the chaos of it all. Failure has become a second skin, fear is rooted deep in our blood, and uncertainty constantly clouds and maims us.

But in every singular moment, an emotion etches itself onto us, and we, in turn, begin to reminisce about these memories.

It’s true. We don’t know anything about life. But we have something beyond it: we feel. We feel and through that, we live. Because at each passing second we spend gracing the trenches of this world, a story can be told. Whether in our brightest moments or our deepest regrets, whether in a sweet lingering memory or a bitter taste of reality, no story is too little or insignificant to be proof of our existence.

And so, as you pick this up, know that within these pages live our emotions, our questions, and the pieces of ourselves we dared to share. Each and every one was crafted not from the knowledge of our minds but the wisdom of our hearts. A mosaic of our past that we form in scribbles and paragraphs, one written and rewritten draft after draft, now woven and collected into a singular book.

We hope you enjoy!
Divya Kurli
